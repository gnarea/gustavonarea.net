TSPD Goes Bimonthly

posted in The Software Practitioner's Digest on March 8, 2012 by Gustavo
SHARE
Tweet

Due to the low volume of relevant resources published during even months, I’ve decided to make The Software Practitioner’s Digest a bimonthly periodical starting with the March/April 2012 issue to be published in early May.

Looking through the notes I took for the publications I read in February, I couldn’t really find anything relevant for the next digest and therefore there won’t be a digest for February.

The reason for the scarce amount of relevant contents in even months is the fact that the periodicals I normally highlight are published in odd months. Relevant contents produced by other sources are highly infrequent, so it makes more sense to adopt the periodicity of the main sources.
